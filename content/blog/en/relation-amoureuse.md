**In a love relationship there are three entities to nurture: You, Your partner, Your couple.**

"The perfect relationship, like the perfect partner, does not exist: it is created."

Here are six key recommendations for creating and maintaining a nurturing love relationship.

---

## 1. Schedule couple time every week

Once a week, block off time in your calendar for the couple — to enjoy quality time together, without interruptions from children, family or friends.

## 2. Establish shared projects

Develop common goals for your relationship, your family, humanity, or your impact in the world.

## 3. Communicate openly

Speak openly about all aspects of your life — without blame, accusation or guilt. Transparency is essential for nurturing the relationship.

## 4. Prioritize physical intimacy

Address sexual health, particularly for women raised with repressive attitudes toward sexuality.

> "Sexual energy has, among other things, a self-healing power."

## 5. Commit fully

"Being in a relationship means making a conscious choice" and accepting the consequences. As Collette Portelance puts it:

> "It is not commitment that imprisons you in a couple, it is doubt and indecision."

## 6. Seek professional help

Ask for help during difficult periods, when love is still present, setting the ego aside.

---

A nurturing relationship does not fall from the sky. It is built, week after week, with intention, courage and love.
