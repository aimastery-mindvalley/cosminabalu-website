I had the opportunity to attend a conference organized by Caroline Codsi on **"Women in Governance"** with Maggie Henriquez, CEO at KRUG (LVMH) in France.

Maggie Henriquez is a woman who "turns companies around brilliantly and enables them to emerge from crisis stronger than ever."

Here are the lessons I took from it, organized in three dimensions.

---

## The Career Woman

- Love giving to others
- Have an enormous social conscience
- Know and understand the company's culture
- Have a deep desire to find quick solutions
- Find a unique way of presenting information
- Accept mistakes with humility
- Stay authentic and true to your values
- Set healthy boundaries
- Work with clients
- Develop products adapted to the crisis
- Do not feed the ego

## The Mother

- Do not feel guilty
- Do things with common sense and love
- Understand your children
- Listen

## The Visionary

- Magic does not exist, you must work with awareness
- Do not stray from your values
- Work according to values: Respect, Gratitude, Generosity, Depth
- Be responsible for your life and your team

> "The key to growth is not to have a vision of the future, but to live fully what you are doing today."

---

I am deeply grateful to these inspiring women who are contributing to changing the future of women — one conference, one conversation, one decision at a time.
